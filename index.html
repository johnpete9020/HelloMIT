From Your Notes (core facts)

  • Power: \( (x^n)'=n x^{n-1} \).
  • Exponential: \( (e^{rx})'=r e^{rx} \), \( (a^x)'=a^x\ln a \).
  • Trig: \( (\sin x)'=\cos x \), \( (\cos x)'=-\sin x \), \( (\tan x)'=\sec^2 x \).
  • Inverse trig: \( (\tan^{-1}x)'=\dfrac{1}{1+x^2} \).
  • Log: \( (\ln x)'=\dfrac{1}{x} \), and \( (\ln(ax))'=\dfrac{1}{x} \) for \( a>0 \).
  • Linear combos: \( (f+g)'=f'+g' \), \( (c f)'=c f' \).
  • Product: \( (fg)'=f'g+fg' \).
  • Quotient: \( \left(\dfrac{f}{g}\right)'=\dfrac{f'g-fg'}{g^2} \).
  • Chain: \( (f\circ g)'=f'(g(x))\,g'(x) \).
